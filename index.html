Our Mission

To advance, develop and protect the rights of adolescent girls and women

Our Vision

A future where women and girls are able to fully exercise their rights in an open and inclusive society

Adolescent Girls and Womenâ€™s Rights Forum (AGWRF) is a women and girlsâ€™ rights focused grass root organisation established in 2019 by a group of young women coming together to defend, advance and protect women and girls especially in marginalised and disenfranchised communities in their diversity to fully enjoy their human rights. The organization operates in Midlands and part of Masvingo province. It envisions a future where women and girls are able to fully exercise their rights in an open and inclusive society. The mission and vision are informed by personal experiences of founders, community needs assessment and gaps within national policies and community and national development discourses. AGWRF was formed after the realization that women and girls in their diversity are being left out in the empowerment and development discourses. The organization influences policies at national level as it relates to the rights of women and girls in their diversity and advance the rights based and pro-people approach to social and economic empowerment. AGWRF is there to create and facilitate space for women and girls to have a collective voice to challenge structural barriers and participate in key decision making processes.
